Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 10 AM (subject to availability). Next-day delivery is available for most orders, and we can often accommodate rush requests - just give us a call.

We deliver throughout Northwest Indiana including Gary, Hammond, East Chicago, Merrillville, Crown Point, Hobart, Portage, Valparaiso, and Calumet City. We also serve parts of the Chicago Southland. Contact us for deliveries outside this area.

Yes! We offer Net-30 payment terms for approved contractors. Apply for a contractor account by contacting us with your business information and trade references. Approval typically takes 1-2 business days.

We stock #1 and #2 grade lumber, premium select boards, treated lumber for outdoor use, and specialty hardwoods. All our products meet or exceed industry standards. Let us know your project requirements and we'll recommend the right grade.

Yes! We offer free log pickup service. If you have logs or large timber you need removed, we'll haul them away at no charge. Logs must be 8' and at least ~85% straight for pickup. We use this material for milling and firewood. Contact us to schedule a pickup.

We stand behind our products. Unused materials in original condition can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Custom cuts and special orders are non-returnable. If you receive damaged goods, contact us immediately for a replacement.
