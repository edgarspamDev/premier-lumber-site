About Premier Lumber

Family-owned. Community-focused. Quality-driven.

Our Story

Built on Hard Work & Family Values

Premier Lumber Co started with a simple idea: provide quality wood products and honest service to our community. What began as a small pallet operation has grown into a full-service lumber company serving businesses and homeowners throughout Northwest Indiana.

As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of relationships. When you call Premier Lumber, you're not just a number — you're a neighbor. We take pride in knowing our customers by name and going the extra mile to meet their needs.

Why Us

What Sets Us Apart

Family-Owned

Not a corporate chain. We're your neighbors, and we treat every customer like family.

Quality Materials

Every product is inspected and handled with care before it leaves our yard.

Local Delivery

We deliver throughout NW Indiana. Ask about scheduling when you call.

Wood Recycling

We recycle and repurpose wood materials, reducing waste and supporting sustainability.

Partnerships

Working with Tree Service Companies

We work with local tree service partners when needed. If your crew removes hardwood trees in the NW Indiana area, we accept qualifying logs at our yard or can arrange regular pickups from your job sites. You save on disposal costs. We get quality raw material. Everyone wins.
