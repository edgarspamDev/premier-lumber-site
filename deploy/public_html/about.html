About Premier Lumber

Family-owned. Community-focused. Quality-driven.

Our Story

Built on Hard Work & Family Values

Premier Lumber Co started with a simple idea: provide quality wood products and honest service to our community. What began as a small pallet operation has grown into a full-service lumber company serving businesses and homeowners throughout the Chicago Southland and Northwest Indiana.

As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of relationships. When you call Premier Lumber, you're not just a number—you're a neighbor. We take pride in knowing our customers by name and going the extra mile to meet their needs.

Our commitment to quality has never wavered. Every pallet we build, every cord of firewood we deliver, meets our high standards. We believe in doing things right the first time, every time.
