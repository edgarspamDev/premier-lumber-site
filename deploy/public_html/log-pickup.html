We Pick Up Your Hardwood Logs

Have fallen trees or cleared hardwood logs on your property? Instead of paying to haul them away, call us. We pick up qualifying logs at no charge and repurpose them into lumber and firewood.

Requirements

What We Accept

  • Hardwood onlyOak, ash, maple, walnut, cherry, hickory, elm
  • Minimum 14" diameterMeasured at the widest point
  • Minimum 8' lengthLonger logs preferred
