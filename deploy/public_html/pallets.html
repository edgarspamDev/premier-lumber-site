Pallets Built for Your Business

At Premier Lumber, we understand that your business depends on reliable pallets. Whether you're shipping products across the country or storing inventory in your warehouse, our pallets are built to handle the job.

What We Offer

Types of Pallets

New Custom Pallets

Built to your exact specifications using quality lumber. Perfect for specialized shipping needs, heavy loads, or specific size requirements.

  • Any size or configuration
  • Choice of wood species
  • Block or stringer design
  • ISPM-15 compliant options

Recycled Pallets

Quality inspected and repaired pallets at a fraction of the cost. Great for domestic shipping, storage, and one-way shipments.

  • Cost-effective solution
  • Inspected for quality
  • Various grades available
  • Eco-friendly choice

Heat-Treated Pallets

ISPM-15 certified heat-treated pallets required for international shipping. Meets all export regulations for wood packaging.

  • ISPM-15 certified
  • Required for export
  • Stamped and documented
  • Standard & custom sizes

Heavy-Duty Pallets

Reinforced pallets designed for heavy loads and demanding applications. Built with extra stringers and thicker lumber.

  • Up to 5,000 lb capacity
  • Reinforced construction
  • Industrial applications
  • Long-lasting durability
