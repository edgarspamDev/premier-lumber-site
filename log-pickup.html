Free Service

Got Logs? We'll Take Them!

Whether you're a tree service with logs from a removal job or a homeowner cleaning up after a storm, we'll come pick up your logs for free. It's a win-win: you get rid of the logs, and we turn them into pallets, firewood, and other useful products.

This service is part of our commitment to sustainability and reducing waste. Instead of those logs ending up in a landfill, they get a second life as quality wood products.

Log Requirements

What We Accept

We DO Accept

  • Hardwood logs (oak, maple, ash, etc.)
  • Softwood logs (pine, spruce, etc.)
  • Logs 6" diameter or larger
  • Storm-downed trees
  • Tree removal logs
  • Logs with bark
  • Green or seasoned wood

We DON'T Accept

  • Brush, branches, or limbs
  • Stumps or root balls
  • Treated or painted wood
  • Rotted or hollow logs
  • Logs with excessive nails/hardware
  • Construction debris
  • Logs smaller than 6" diameter

Not Sure If We'll Take Your Logs?

No problem! Just give us a call or send a photo. We're happy to let you know if your logs qualify for free pickup. In some cases, we may be able to work with you even if the logs don't meet our standard requirements.
