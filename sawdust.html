Applications

What Can You Use Sawdust For?

Our sawdust and shavings have countless practical uses.

Animal Bedding

Perfect for horse stables, chicken coops, rabbit hutches, and other animal housing. Absorbent, comfortable, and easy to clean.

  • Highly absorbent
  • Comfortable for animals
  • Chemical-free & safe

Garden & Landscaping

Use as mulch around plants, garden pathways, or to improve soil composition. Helps retain moisture and suppress weeds.

  • Natural mulch alternative
  • Pathway material
  • Soil amendment

Industrial Uses

Excellent for oil and liquid absorption in workshops and factories. Also used for packing, ice traction, and floor drying.

  • Oil spill absorption
  • Workshop floor covering
  • Packing material

Composting

Add carbon-rich sawdust to your compost pile to balance nitrogen-heavy kitchen scraps. Creates rich, healthy compost.

  • Carbon source for compost
  • Balances green material
  • Improves compost texture
